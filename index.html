About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research

This course explores the practical applications of Generative AI in professional and research contexts. We'll examine how AI tools can enhance productivity, streamline workflows, and unlock new possibilities in data analysis and research methodologies.

Through hands-on projects and case studies, we'll learn to leverage AI for tasks ranging from data processing and visualization to content creation and problem-solving in specialized domains like public health and epidemiology.

Course Syllabus

Course Highlights

  • Understanding Generative AI fundamentals
  • AI applications in research and data analysis
  • Prompt engineering for specialized domains
  • Ethical considerations in AI implementation
  • Integrating AI tools into existing workflows
  • Case studies in public health applications
  • Hands-on projects with real-world data
  • Future trends in AI for research
